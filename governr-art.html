FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is governr?

governr is an AI risk and oversight platform for enterprises that need continuous visibility across internal and third-party AI. It maintains a living AI inventory, applies contextual risk scoring across apps, agents, models, and plugins, and continuously monitors change so your evidence stays current.

What counts as an AI asset?

Anything in your organisation that creates AI exposure or makes AI-driven decisions: models, agents, AI-enabled workflows, vendor tools with embedded AI, prompt repositories, datasets used by AI, and API integrations that send data to AI services.

How does governr discover shadow and vendor AI?

governr maps your estate through read-only, API-led connections to the systems you already run, then layers in vendor and third-party signals. It surfaces the AI that never went through a formal approval, including agentic tools and embedded vendor models.

How long does it take to get value?

Most firms have a live Atlas within two hours and find their first surprise inside the first hour. No six-month programme, and no changes to your infrastructure.

Which frameworks does governr map to?

15+ regulatory frameworks including the EU AI Act, DORA, FCA, FINRA, MAS, SEC, OCC, HIPAA, ISO 42001, and OWASP, with mappings auto-updated as obligations evolve.
