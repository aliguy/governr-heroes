The blind spot

You have a policy. You don't have proof.

We spent this year inside regulated firms, sitting with CROs and CTOs. The same three problems came up every time.

01

You can't see the estate

One firm told us they ran five AI systems. We mapped 131. Roughly 60-70% of what's running is invisible to the people accountable for it.

02

Assurance is a snapshot

A system gets signed off, the paperwork freezes, then it drifts. A point-in-time certificate is not live visibility into what is running now.

03

Risk is argued, not priced

Ten thousand alerts, none of them joined up, and AI risk debated on opinion. Until exposure carries a financial number, the board can't weigh it.
